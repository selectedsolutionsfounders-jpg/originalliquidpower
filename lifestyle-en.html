A closer look at the ingredients inside The Original Liquid Power

Our premium energy drink is formulated with the highest quality ingredients. Discover how each component of our 1991 original formula works to deliver sustained energy, enhanced performance, and an unmistakable taste.

Caffeine (80 mg): The engine behind your focus

Caffeine is the premier cognitive stimulant. Each 250 ml can of The Original Liquid Power delivers 80 mg of caffeine — the optimal dose studied to block adenosine receptors in the brain. This translates to an immediate reduction in perceived fatigue, heightened alertness, and a measurable improvement in reaction times and concentration. Ideal for studying, working, or training hard.

Taurine: Muscular performance and recovery

Taurine is a fundamental amino acid that occurs naturally in the human body. In our energy drink, taurine works in synergy with caffeine to enhance cardiovascular and muscular performance. It also plays a key role in maintaining cellular electrolyte balance and supports post-exertion recovery by reducing oxidative stress.

B Vitamin Complex (B3, B5, B6 & B12)

B vitamins are essential for normal energy metabolism. Niacin (B3) and Pantothenic Acid (B5) convert nutrients into cellular energy. Vitamin B6 supports protein and glycogen metabolism, while Vitamin B12 is critical for red blood cell formation, nervous system function, and a significant reduction in fatigue and tiredness.

Carbohydrates: Fast, absorbable energy

For an effective and immediate energy boost, the original formula includes the perfect ratio of sugars and glucose. These simple carbohydrates provide the primary fuel your muscles and brain demand during periods of intense physical or mental strain, ensuring a rapid replenishment of glycogen stores without any heaviness.

Sparkling Water & Citric Acid: A classic, unchanged flavor

Premium sparkling water serves as the ideal vehicle for the rapid absorption of active ingredients, delivering a clean mouthfeel. Citric acid acts as a natural preservative and masterfully balances the sweetness, creating the iconic, refreshing flavor profile of The Original Liquid Power — instantly recognizable to our consumers from the very first sip.
